Emma Liano creates crazy cats

Hello, my name is Emma Liano and I am a student at the Cleveland Institue of Art. My intended major is Graphic Design and I plan to run my own printing and graphics shop when I am out of school. But for now my passion is creating cats.

My cats are inspired by a book series "Warriors", and these characters are all of my own design and each have their own personalities. They are what really got me into digital art and drawing things in general. I still have a lot to work on but hopefully they get me far.
